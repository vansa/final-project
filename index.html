ABOUT ME

Humanoid. Gif Connoisseur. Packaging Horder. Dessert Lover.

I'm from Springfield, VA - right outside of DC. From as early as i can remember I've always had an affinity for colors, crafts and various creative mediums. My mom recalls me demanding play-doh and crayons whenever we'd go, and in lieu of a traditional "Quinceanera" I asked for a computer instead. We've been inseparable since.
